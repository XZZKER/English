# 名词性从句

- 从句是为了表达简单句难于表达的想法
- 主语从句、表语从句、宾语从句、同位语从句
- 名词性从句 = 用句子代替简单句中的名词短语

## 如何学号从句

1. 把从句部分看作一个简单句，并对应五种基本句型之一
2. 找出从句的主谓宾定状补
3. 判断连词（关系词）在从句中承担何种成分
4. 重新理顺从句部分的语序

## 独立句子转为名词性从句的具体步骤

1. 调语序，调整为陈述语序
2. 加连词，陈述句用that，一般疑问句用whether，特殊疑问句用原本的疑问词
3. 把连词置于从句句首

##### 陈述句：

1. That he is a transsexual doesn't matter.
2. I don't know that he is a transsexual.
3. The problem is that he is a transsexual.

##### 一般疑问句：

1. Whether he is a transsexual doesn't matter.
2. I don't know whether he is a transsexual.
3. The problem is whether he is a transsexual.

##### 特殊疑问句：

1. What he is doesn't matter. 
2. I don't know what he is.
3. The problem is what he is.

## 同位语从句

- 同位语 = 针对抽象名词的名词性解释说明

##### 例如：

1. The news, that they won the championship, spread quickly.
2. The fact, that she lied, disappointed everyone.
3. I have no idea what has happened to him.

## 疑问词作主语，无需变更语序

##### 例如：

1. I don't know what happened yesterday.
2. I don't know who is in the classroom.

## 形式主语

- EndWeight，头轻尾重

##### 可行，但不常用：

1. That Sally is beautiful is true.
2. Whether Sally is beautiful doesn't matter.
3. Why Sally is beautiful doesn't matter.

##### 常用：

1. **It**'s true that Sally is beautiful.
2. **It** doesn't matter whether Sally is beautiful.
3. **It** doesn't matter why Sally is beautiful.

## 形式宾语

- EndWeight，头轻尾重
- 只能使用EndWeight原则，否则错误

##### 例如：

1. I find it unbelievable that Sally is beautiful.
2. Our teacher made it a rule that we have to speak English in class.

## that的省略

- 省略问题的原则就是不影响理解就能省略

##### 例如：

1. I don't know he is a transsexual.
2. The problem is he is a transsexual.
