# 连词

- 简单句 + 简单句，必须有连词

## run-on 和 comma splice

- 一个逗号前，只能有一套简单句结构，除非使用连词
- 一个句号前，只能有一个谓语动词，除非使用连词

##### 普通句：

1. I love you. You love me.
2. Tommy loves fish. Floppy loves meat.
3. Michael is a boy. Sally is a girl.

##### Run-on Sentence（连缀句）：

1. I love you you love me.
2. Tommy loves fish Floppy loves meat.
3. Michael is a boy Sally is a girl.

##### Comma splice（逗号粘连）：

1. I love you, you love me.
2. Tommy loves fish, Floppy loves meat.
3. Michael is a boy, Sally is a girl.

##### 正确写法：

1. I love you, and you love me.
2. Tommy loves fish, but Floppy loves meat.
3. Michael is a boy, and Sally is a girl.

## 连词 + 简单句 = 并列结构 or 主从结构

##### 并列结构：

1. She enjoys painting, and he loves playing the guitar.
2. The sun was shining brightly, but dark clouds were gathering on the horizon.

##### 主从结构：

1. What she said about the incident surprised everyone.
2. He believes that honesty is the best policy.
3. The important thing is that we tried our best.
4. The fact that she won the competition delighted her family.
5. After she finished her homework, she want for a walk.

| which     | n, adj |

| why       | adv    |
| how       | adv    |
